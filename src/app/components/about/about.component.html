what we do

SendIT is a courier service that helps users deliver parcels to different destinations. SendIT provides courier quotes based on weight categories.
SendIT app connects you with a broad range of different cities.We help our clients deliver or receive their packages from wherever in the World.

our history

SendIT company was founded in 2022 by Chanai Agwata, Michelle Njeri, Maureen Mwangi, Ivy Jeptoo, Caleb Kabaya and Kimberly Olanga.The company began delivery in the same year with the launch of different commodities. SendIT has been known so far for delivering in time and for the food security and confidentiality of our parcels.

our mission

SendIT is on a mission to make deliveries effortless for everyone, everywhere. Our service connects customers to speed delivery from over 80 cities around the world. We give people more options when choosing what to deliver and how to deliver their parcels. We help our clients deliver their packages and build their trust in us.
